[COLOR blue][B]Free Flow v1.0.4[/B][/COLOR]

🚀 [B]Initial Release – Non-Debrid Focused Build[/B]

Free Flow launches as a streamlined, no-nonsense addon built specifically for users who want a smooth experience without relying on premium services. This version focuses on speed, simplicity, and accessibility while laying the groundwork for future expansion.

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
🧩 Added Free Flow Live Section.

🔓 [B]Non-Debrid One Click System[/B]
A fully integrated one-click setup designed for free sources only. No accounts, no authorisation steps—just select and play. Everything is tuned for quick access and minimal user input.

🧹 [B]Scrapers Removed[/B]
All traditional scraper modules have been stripped out. This reduces overhead, eliminates unnecessary delays, and avoids common scraping failures—resulting in a more stable and predictable experience.

🚫 [B]Premium Services Removed[/B]
Support for premium providers (Real-Debrid, Premiumize, AllDebrid, etc.) has been completely removed. Free Flow is intentionally built to operate independently of paid services.

🎬 [B]Optimised Free Link Selection[/B]
Sources are prioritised to deliver the best possible quality available without a premium account. The addon intelligently focuses on reliable, working links to reduce buffering and dead sources.

⚡️ [B]Fast & Lightweight Performance[/B]
With fewer dependencies and a trimmed-down backend, navigation is quicker, menus load faster, and playback starts with minimal delay.

🧩 [B]Clean & Simplified Structure[/B]
The addon has been organised for ease of use, making it ideal for both new users and streamlined builds. No clutter, no unnecessary configuration.


🚧 [B]Ongoing Development[/B]
New content sections, categories, and enhancements are already in development and will be rolled out in upcoming updates.

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

💡 [B]Summary[/B]
Free Flow v1.0.0 delivers a focused, plug-and-play experience—fast, stable, and built entirely around free streaming with zero setup required.
